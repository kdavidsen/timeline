Birth of Garamont

1480

Garamont was born in 1480 to Yvon Garamont and Francoise Barbier, both of whom were from printer families.

The Font Garamond

1538

Garamond is characterized by soft, rounded serifs, medium contrast, and flag-shaped top serifs.

Jean Jannon

1580 - 1658

Jean Jannon is one of the people credited for the modern Garamond typeface.

Didot

1790

Didot takes over as the dominent font for the 19th century.

Industiralization of Typesetting

1814

Friedrich Koenig invents the steam powered printing press revolutionizing the printing process.

Modern Type

1929 -

Teletype setting marks the start of typesetting on a screen.

Birth of Frutiger

1928

Frutiger was born in Unterseen, Canton of Bern. While young, he experimented with invented scripts and eventually appreticed at the Otto Schlaefli printing house in Interlaken.

OCR-B

1968

Frutiger creates OCR-B which became the world standard in 1973. It's used for machine reading while still being easier for the human eye.

The Font Avenir

1988

Avenir is characterized by large counters, sharp angles, and mostly its humanist geometric look.
